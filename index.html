Mixed Martial Arts

Mixed martial arts (MMA) is a combat sport that combines various techniques and disciplines from different martial arts and combat sports. It allows fighters to use a wide range of striking and grappling techniques, both standing and on the ground. MMA has gained significant popularity around the world and is often associated with organizations like the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC)
